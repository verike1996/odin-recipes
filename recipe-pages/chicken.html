Chicken

Description

Seasoned Chicken that can go with just about any side. My favorite is rice and broccoli!

Ingredients:

  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast
  • Spices
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Heat a drizzle of oil in a pan on medium-high heat.
  2. While that's heating, break the tendons in your chicken and trim the fat.
  3. Coat each side of your chicken breast with garlic powder, paprika, italian seasoning, and whatever other spices you like.
  4. Gently place the coated chicken breast in the pan of hot oil.
  5. Cook until internal temperature has reached at least 165Â°F.
  6. Enjoy!
