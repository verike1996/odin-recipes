Chicken

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Bowl
  • Cereal
  • Spoon

Steps

  1. Grab your bowl.
  2. Pour Cereal into your bowl.
  3. Add milk to your cereal (DO NOT PUT MILK IN THE BOWL BEFORE THE CEREAL, YOU WILL RUIN THE CEREAL)
  4. Put your spoon in the bowl filled with cereal and milk.
  5. Enjoy!
